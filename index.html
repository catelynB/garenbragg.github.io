About Garen:

Garen is an up-and-coming software developer in Indiana, transitioning from a sales background. At the age of 17 Garen enlisted in the Army National Guard as a private in the Infantry. After his initial training Garen attended Purdue University College of Technology where he graduated in 2016. Upon graduation Garen accepted a commission as an officer in Army Ordnance Corps. In his civilian career Garen became a salesman, first selling insurance and then selling for third-party logistics services. In 2019 he was deployed to the Middle East where he led a munitions team in Iraq and Kuwait. After leading a successful mission, and with all his Soldiers safe back in the USA, Garen enrolled in Eleven Fifty Academy to broaden his horizons and continue to solve interesting problems.
